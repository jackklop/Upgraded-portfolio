About us

We are dedicated to serving our customers with care by serving great food and providing good service for the past 20 years. Our authentic recipes comes from chefs that has worked in China for over a decade and decided to bring this wonderful culture to America. We serve all kind of seafood; lobster, crab, fish, shrimp, oytser, and clam!

Special Hours

We open for dinner mainly, from 4pm to 3am. We tend to be busiest from 6-8, to guarenteed a seat please call before hand to reserve a table. In addition, We do serve alcohol, must show ID over 18.

Efficent

We always try to keep customers waiting as little as possible! We know the feeling of hungriess and aimlessly waiting for the food to arrive. That is why we train our staff to be efficent without detering the quailty of the dish.

Fresh

Our seafoods are fresh. You can see them in water tanks on display. We choose from the best of the best, inorder to bring you a great unforgetable experience.
